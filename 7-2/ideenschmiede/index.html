Ideenschmiede
How can a city become more attractive to students, technical talents, creative talents and graduates? What kind of conditions should a city satisfy to inspire the creativity of talents and become the driving force and attraction of urban development? In this project, we look for development potential and characteristics of Bremen and find new solutions for the future creative urban development.
In addition, in this project, the container would be used as our design medium. Due to its low construction cost and rapid construction, container can be used to offer a creative environment for cities with rising living costs and thus attract more creative talents. We hope that the Ideenschmiede can provide a place for Bremen, where culture, knowledge, creativity and experience can be exchanged.

Why Choose Rembertiring?

With the development of the city, the space of the city has become more compact, and the price of land and housing has also increased, which is often contradictory to the construction of the creative city. We need to find new possibilities in the city and use the urban space more effectively. After field research of entire Bremen, we finally chose Rembertiring as our design site. Rembertiring is a traffic roundabout where residents sometimes walk around and have a picnic, which is a very important city node. Next we will have further research on this place.

Location Research

Rembertiring connects the central railway station, Viertel district, Dobben district, and the city centre. These important areas of Bremen provide good nutrient transport, rich infrastructure and diverse cultural services for Rembertiring.

With the development of the city, land resources are becoming scarcer. The Rembertiring is about 90 meters in diameter, so it can provide about 6,000 square meters of extra space for the city.

Because Rembertiring is a traffic roundabout, the traffic around it is very complicated and there is some noise during the morning and evening peaks. Sometimes residents around will go for a walk and have a rest.

The dense trees of Rembertiring provide good living conditions for this place and play a role in reducing noise.

Services Provided by Ideenschmieide

We use two layers of containers as the main building. Under the main body of the building, use a metal bracket to lift the entire building body. This will reduce the construction cost, and the design of the bottom space will not be limited by the size of the container.

Since the main body of the building is made up of containers, the container also has obvious shortcomings, bad heat insulation and bad sound insulation. We use wood grille for the exterior walls of the building. On the one hand, it enhances the overall beauty of the building, reduces the industrial feel of the container, and integrates the entire building into the Rembertiring environment. On the other hand, the wood strips can help maintain a relatively balanced temperature within the building and also provide some soundproofing functionality.
