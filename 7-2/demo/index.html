Demo Mode
Creative Lead & UX Designer
Along with the development of vehicle intelligence and the rapid iteration of OTAs, two problems have been created: 1. It is difficult for the owner to quickly understand and be familiar with the intelligent application on the car, as well as some details of use. 2. For salespeople, it is difficult to describe the highlight functions of the vehicle to consumers in a short period of time.
In response to these two pain points, Ford hopes to solve these problems through the “Demo Mode” on-board application. Demo Mode is divided into two modules: 1. Tutorial: Help car owners understand the latest intelligent functions, and the content in the video will be iterated with OTA. 2. Pre-purchase Video: Show the vehicle’s highlight functions to potential users and the public as a tool for sales and marketing.

What features do we need to cover in the video?

A Workshop was held to understand the needs of different departments within Ford and unify the direction of the design. During Workshop empathy map was used as our tool. Understand the psychological needs of users through three different stages: 1. Before buying a car. 2. New to get the car. 3.OTA. In the end, we picked out 9 functions according to different dimensions

What is difference between storytelling way of Pre-purchase and Tutorial

Pre-purchase video is primarily aimed at potential purchasers. We have to leave an impression on the user in a minute. So the video style is abstract and can deepen the brand impression; while the tutorial video is aimed at users who already own a car, and the video style will be more rational and organized. The narration logic of the video is consistent, which is, the function introduction, the use scenario, and how to operate.

How to make a rapid iteration of the video early in the project?

In the early days of video production, except for storyboard, text script and different interfaces were used to communicate with different Feature Owners from Ford to ensure a correct understanding of the function and the design concept at its core. When making a video Sample, we will use text-to-speech in advance to ensure a certain degree of realism and reduce the workload of modifying video clips later.
